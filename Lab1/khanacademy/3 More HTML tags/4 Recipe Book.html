Recipe #1: Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground beef 500g
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

Step 2: In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.

Step 3: Add the ground beef and cook until browned.

Step 4: Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 5: Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Recipe #2: Chicken Curry

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 500g, diced
Coconut milk 1 can
Curry powder 2 tbsp
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced

Step 1: In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.

Step 2: Add the chicken and cook until browned.

Step 3: Stir in the curry powder and cook for 1 minute.

Step 4: Add the coconut milk and simmer for 30 minutes.

Step 5: Serve with rice.

Recipe #3: Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1.5 cups
Cocoa powder 3/4 cup
Baking powder 1.5 tsp
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients together.

Step 3: Add the eggs and milk, and mix until smooth.

Step 4: Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.

Step 5: Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
